Value Pricing

Mystudytools value pricing is based upon our guiding principle. We promise to deliver to you the best possible academic writing for your project.
 

The professional writing we provide is difficult and time-consuming. It demands high-level compositional skills and a dedication to providing you with writing you can stand behind.

For standard academic projects, we deliver approximately 300 words per page, double-spaced, Times Roman 12pt font. Other formats (scripts, speeches, PowerPoint, mathematical calculations), per page charges may vary slightly

 

Three factors influence our pricing on your project

Your deadline. We guarantee to meet it or beat it.
The minimum number of pages required. We never come up short.
Your academic level. We don’t “under-write” or “over-write.”
